# 💳 Credit Card Financial Analysis Dashboard | SQL + Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **Credit Card Financial Data** using **SQL** for data extraction & transformation and **Power BI** for interactive dashboard creation.

The objective of this project is to help financial organizations understand customer spending behavior, transaction patterns, and revenue generation in order to make better business decisions and improve profitability.

---

# 🛠️ Tech Stack

- SQL
- Power BI
- Data Cleaning
- Data Modeling
- DAX
- Data Visualization

---

# 📊 Dashboard 1 : Credit Card Transaction Report

This dashboard provides insights into transaction performance and revenue generation.

### Key KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Interest Earned
- Total Transaction Amount
- Total Transaction Count

### Business Insights

- Revenue trend across different quarters
- Transaction volume analysis
- Revenue by Card Category
- Revenue by Expenditure Type
- Revenue by Customer Job
- Revenue by Education Level
- Revenue generated using Chip, Swipe and Online transactions

# 📈 Key Business Insights

### 1️⃣ Blue Credit Card dominates the market
Blue Card contributes approximately **46M revenue**, significantly higher than other card categories. This indicates the company's major customer base belongs to Blue Card holders.

---

### 2️⃣ Bills and Entertainment generate maximum spending

Customers spend the highest amount on

- Bills
- Entertainment
- Fuel
- Grocery

These categories contribute the majority of company revenue.

---

### 3️⃣ Business Professionals are the highest revenue contributors

Business professionals generate nearly **17M revenue**, making them the most valuable customer segment.

---

### 4️⃣ Graduate customers generate the highest revenue

Graduate customers contribute over **22M revenue**, suggesting educated customers actively use credit cards.

---

### 5️⃣ High Income customers generate maximum revenue

Customers in the High Income segment contribute almost **22M revenue**, indicating premium customers should be targeted with personalized offers.

---

# 💼 Business Recommendations

✅ Introduce premium reward programs for Blue Card customers.

✅ Offer cashback on Bills, Entertainment and Fuel to increase customer retention.

✅ Target Business Professionals and High Income customers with exclusive premium credit cards.

✅ Expand marketing campaigns in top-performing states to maximize revenue.

✅ Improve digital payment incentives since online transactions are much lower than swipe transactions.
